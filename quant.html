策略开发生命周期

量化训练的核心不是寻找万能指标，而是把想法变成可测试、可解释、可复盘的研究流程。

01提出假设

写清楚策略想捕捉的市场行为，例如趋势延续、均值回归或波动扩张。

02定义数据

确认品种、周期、数据质量、缺失值处理和样本区间。

03编码执行逻辑

把入场、出场、仓位、止损、过滤条件和异常处理写成明确规则。

04回测与压力测试

观察交易次数、最大回撤、盈亏分布、参数敏感性和样本外表现。

05风险评审

识别过拟合、滑点、流动性、极端行情和实盘执行差异。

06复盘迭代

保留研究日志，记录为什么修改、修改后改变了什么、哪些假设仍未被验证。

Strategy Development Lifecycle

The core of quantitative training is not finding a universal indicator. It is turning an idea into a testable, explainable and reviewable research process.

01State the Hypothesis

Describe the market behaviour the strategy aims to capture, such as trend continuation, mean reversion or volatility expansion.

02Define the Data

Confirm instruments, timeframe, data quality, missing-value handling and sample period.

03Code Execution Logic

Translate entry, exit, sizing, stop, filter conditions and exception handling into explicit rules.

04Backtest and Stress Test

Review trade count, drawdown, return distribution, parameter sensitivity and out-of-sample behaviour.

05Risk Review

Identify overfitting, slippage, liquidity, extreme regimes and live-execution gaps.

06Review and Iterate

Keep a research log explaining why changes were made, what changed and which assumptions remain untested.

EA 系统架构

EA 不是一个“下单按钮”，而是一套分层交易系统

课程把 MT5 EA 拆成信号、风控、订单、日志和异常处理五个层级，让学员知道代码每一层承担什么责任。

层级训练内容复盘重点
数据输入读取价格、周期、指标缓存和交易时段，确认数据是否可用。数据完整性

风控引擎定义单笔风险、最大持仓、连续亏损、暂停和禁止交易条件。风险优先

异常防护处理断线、报价异常、点差扩大、新闻时段和超出交易窗口的情况。异常边界

回测真实性边界

回测是研究工具，不是未来收益证明。专业训练要让学员知道回测能回答什么，也要知道它不能回答什么。

回测可以帮助回答回测不能证明课程训练动作
规则在历史样本中是否产生可观察行为。策略的未来有效性，或它在任何市场状态下都有效。分样本、分市场状态记录表现。
参数变化是否导致结果大幅敏感。找到一个“最佳参数”就代表找到真实规律。做参数敏感性表和样本外观察。
交易次数、回撤、盈亏分布和连续亏损是否可接受。最大回撤不会在未来被突破。加入压力测试和暂停规则。
点差、佣金、滑点假设对结果有多大影响。模拟成交等同于真实成交。记录执行成本假设和异常行情说明。
策略假设是否值得继续研究。模型可以替代交易纪律和风险管理。写策略假设、失效条件和版本日志。

Truth Boundaries of Backtesting

A backtest is a research tool, not proof of future outcomes. Professional training should teach what backtests can answer and what they cannot prove.

Backtests Can Help AnswerBacktests Cannot ProveCourse Training Action
Whether rules produced observable behaviour in historical samples.Future effectiveness or effectiveness across every regime.Record performance by sample and market regime.
Whether parameter changes create material sensitivity.That one optimal parameter represents a real market law.Build parameter sensitivity and out-of-sample checks.
Whether trade count, drawdown, distribution and losing streaks are tolerable.That future drawdown will not exceed historical drawdown.Add stress tests and pause rules.
How spreads, commissions and slippage assumptions affect results.That simulated execution equals live execution.Document cost assumptions and abnormal market conditions.
Whether the strategy hypothesis deserves further research.That a model can replace discipline and risk management.Write assumptions, invalidation conditions and version logs.
Python 研究台

把 Python 训练做成研究台，而不是零散脚本

Python 课程强调数据表结构、实验日志、样本切分、成本假设和复现能力。学员需要能解释每个信号来自哪里、每次修改改变了什么。

Python Research Desk

Make Python training a research desk, not scattered scripts

The Python pathway emphasizes data-table structure, experiment logs, sample splits, cost assumptions and reproducibility. Learners should explain where each signal came from and what each change altered.

01数据表标准

统一时间戳、开高低收、成交量、点差、交易时段和事件标签，先让数据可以被复查。

02特征与信号

把趋势、波动率、突破、均值回归和过滤条件拆成可解释字段，而不是只输出买卖信号。

03样本切分

训练学员理解训练样本、验证样本、样本外观察和时间序列不能随意打乱的原因。

04成本假设

把点差、佣金、滑点、拒单和交易频率写入评估，避免只看毛收益曲线。

05实验记录

每次参数、过滤器、品种或周期变化，都需要记录原因、结果和下一步问题。

01Data Table Standards

Standardize timestamp, open-high-low-close, volume, spread, trading session and event labels so data can be reviewed.

02Features and Signals

Separate trend, volatility, breakout, mean reversion and filters into explainable fields rather than only outputting buy/sell signals.

03Sample Splits

Teach why training, validation, out-of-sample observation and time-series ordering should be handled carefully.

04Cost Assumptions

Include spreads, commission, slippage, rejections and trading frequency so evaluation is not based only on gross curves.

05Experiment Records

Every parameter, filter, instrument or timeframe change should record the reason, result and next research question.

模型治理

量化系统的成熟度，体现在知道何时不能用

我们把模型治理做成训练内容：不是让学员迷信自动化，而是让学员知道模型风险、执行风险和解释风险如何被记录、复核和暂停。

过拟合参数过度贴合历史样本，需要用敏感性、样本外和简单基准对照。
数据泄漏未来信息、重算指标、错误时间戳都可能让测试结果失真。
生存者偏差只看仍然存在或表现突出的样本，会高估策略稳定性。
制度切换利率、波动率、流动性和政策环境变化会让历史关系失效。
执行漂移模拟成交、真实点差、滑点和订单拒绝之间可能存在差距。
版本漂移频繁修改但没有日志，会让模型失去可解释性和复盘价值。
Model Governance

A mature quant system knows when it should not be used

Model governance is part of the training: learners should not worship automation. They should learn how model risk, execution risk and interpretation risk are recorded, reviewed and paused.

OverfittingParameters may fit historical samples too closely; sensitivity, out-of-sample checks and simple benchmarks are needed.
Data LeakageFuture information, recalculated indicators and wrong timestamps can distort test results.
Survivorship BiasUsing only surviving or successful samples can overstate strategy stability.
Regime ShiftChanges in rates, volatility, liquidity and policy regimes can invalidate historical relationships.
Execution DriftSimulated fills may differ from real spreads, slippage and order rejections.
Version DriftFrequent changes without logs reduce explainability and review value.

报告风险复盘

交易系统风险复盘清单

每一个 EA 或 Python 脚本都需要同时说明它能做什么、不能做什么，以及在什么条件下应停止使用。

样本风险测试区间是否过短？是否只覆盖一种市场状态？
参数风险参数轻微变化后结果是否大幅波动？
执行风险点差、滑点、断线、拒单和交易时段是否被记录？
资金风险仓位、最大回撤、连续亏损和暂停规则是否清楚？
解释风险是否把回测结果误读为未来表现？
治理风险是否有版本记录、复盘负责人和变更说明？

Trading System Risk Review Checklist

Every EA or Python script should explain what it can do, what it cannot do and under what conditions it should stop being used.

Sample RiskIs the test period too short? Does it cover only one market regime?
Parameter RiskDo small parameter changes materially alter the result?
Execution RiskAre spreads, slippage, disconnections, rejections and trading hours recorded?
Capital RiskAre sizing, drawdown, losing streak and pause rules clear?
Interpretation RiskIs the backtest being misread as future performance?
Governance RiskAre version records, reviewers and change notes maintained?

每个项目都应形成可以被导师、同学和未来雇主理解的证据链：代码、研究说明、风险说明、测试记录和复盘结论。

Portfolio Evidence

The Quant Academy should produce professional evidence, not “universal strategies”

Each project should create evidence that instructors, peers and future employers can understand: code, research notes, risk statements, test records and review conclusions.

01Research Memo
