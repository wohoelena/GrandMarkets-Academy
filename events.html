五层活动学习系统

活动不是单次讲座，而是一条从研究主题、专业信任、教育交付到持续学习路径的完整体验。

活动学习系统

让每场活动都能被准备、交付、复盘和持续学习

高质量活动要像机构研究会议一样有议题、有材料、有边界、有记录。参与者离开现场时，不只是“听过一次”，而是知道下一步应该阅读、练习或申请哪条学习路径。

01主题起点

用一个明确金融问题作为起点，例如“黄金为何受实际利率影响”或“EA 策略为什么必须做风险限额”。

02参与匹配

了解参与者背景、城市、学习目标和经验水平，减少不匹配参与，保护活动质量。

03会前预热

发送一页预读材料、关键术语、风险提示和准备问题，让现场讨论更专业。

04现场交付

以研究图表、案例拆解、模拟流程和问答索引为主，避免销售化和情绪化表达。

05会后学习

根据参与者目标建议 RG146、交易员训练、量化学院、知识库阅读或下一次活动。

Five-Layer Event Operating System

An event is not a single talk. It is a chain from attention and trust to education delivery and long-term community continuity.

Event Operating System

Prepare, deliver, review and follow up every programme

High-quality events feel like institutional research meetings: clear agenda, reading material, communication boundaries and documented outputs. Participants leave knowing what to read, practise or apply for next.

01Research Question

Use one precise finance question as the starting point, such as why gold reacts to real rates or why an EA strategy needs risk limits.

02Registration Fit

Understand background, city, learning objective and experience level to reduce mismatched attendance and protect session quality.

03Pre-Session Preparation

Send a one-page briefing, key terminology, risk notes and preparation questions before the session.

04Session Delivery

Lead with research charts, case breakdowns, simulation flows and Q&A indexing, avoiding promotional or emotionally charged language.

05Continuation Plan

Suggest RG146, trader training, Quant Academy, library reading or the next event based on participant objectives.

参与者旅程

站在参与者视角，活动页要回答“我为什么来、我会经历什么、我离开后有什么下一步”。

步骤 01被主题吸引

标题要像研究问题，不像广告口号；让专业学习者觉得这场活动值得判断。

步骤 02确认边界

明确活动用于一般金融教育与学习路径沟通，不承诺收益，不提供个人投资建议。

步骤 03申请匹配

用背景、经验和目标做审慎匹配，让不同水平的学习者进入合适活动。

步骤 04会前预读

提供术语、图表、问题和风险提示，降低现场理解成本。

步骤 05现场互动

通过案例、模拟、问答和小组讨论建立专业参与感。

步骤 06会后学习

发送复盘材料和下一步路径，让参与者自然进入课程、社群或阅读路径。

Attendee Journey

From the participant's perspective, the programme answers why to attend, what will happen and what comes next after leaving.

STEP 01Topic Attraction

The title is framed as a research question, not a promotional slogan, so professional learners can judge its relevance.

STEP 02Boundary Check

Clarify that the session is for general financial education and learning-path communication, without return promises or personal advice.

STEP 03Fit Request

Use background, experience and objectives to match learners with suitable sessions.

STEP 04Preparation Brief

Provide terminology, charts, questions and risk notes to reduce live-session friction.

STEP 05Live Interaction

Build professional participation through cases, simulation, Q&A and group discussion.

STEP 06Post-Session Continuity

Send review material and next pathways so participants can naturally continue into courses, community or reading paths.

每场活动的交付物

高质量活动应该留下可阅读、可复盘、可继续学习的材料，让参与者离开现场后仍然知道下一步做什么。

会前简报一页说明本场主题、核心概念、必要背景和推荐阅读，帮助参与者提前进入共同语境。
现场工作表把案例、图表、风险问题和讨论题整理在同一张表中，避免活动变成单向听讲。
问答索引把高频问题归类为监管、产品、市场、技术和职业发展五个方向。
复盘任务会后给出一个小型研究任务，例如写一张产品风险卡或完成一页宏观数据观察表。
后续路径根据参与者目标推荐 RG146、量化实验室、知识库阅读或下一场研究简报。
合规提示所有活动保持一般教育口径，不提供个人投资建议或金融产品推荐。

Deliverables for Every Session

High-quality events should leave materials that can be read, reviewed and continued after the session.

Pre-Session BriefA one-page note covering topic, key concepts, required background and recommended reading.
Live WorksheetCases, charts, risk questions and discussion prompts are organised into one working document.
Q&A IndexFrequent questions are grouped into regulation, products, markets, technology and career development.
Review TaskA small post-session assignment, such as a product risk card or macro observation sheet.
Next PathwayParticipants are directed to RG146, the Quant Lab, library reading or the next research briefing.
Compliance NoteAll sessions remain general education and do not provide personal investment advice or product recommendations.

活动治理与风险提示

活动越像专业学院，越要把边界讲清楚：教育、研究、模拟和社群沟通都不能被误解为个人投资建议。

一般教育口径讨论金融概念、市场结构、监管常识、风险管理和学习路径，不根据个人情况给出买卖判断。
不展示收益诱导避免用收益截图、保证性语言、短期获利故事或情绪化话术吸引报名。
模拟与真实分离交易实验室使用模拟案例和教育目的流程，强调模拟结果不代表真实交易表现。
资料来源优先宏观、监管和产品内容优先引用官方或专业来源，现场说明信息来源与限制。
问答边界把个人化持仓、开户、具体产品推荐问题引导回一般教育、风险因素和专业咨询渠道。
隐私与沟通申请信息仅用于活动匹配、课程沟通和社群通知，不用于提供个性化交易指令。

Event Governance and Risk Notes

A professional academy keeps boundaries clear: education, research, simulation and community communication are not personal investment advice.

General Education FramingDiscuss financial concepts, market structure, regulatory context, risk management and learning pathways without making client-specific trading judgments.
No Return-Led PromotionAvoid return screenshots, guaranteed language, short-term profit stories or emotional persuasion to attract registration.
Simulation SeparationTrading labs use simulated cases and education workflows, with clear notes that simulated results do not represent live trading outcomes.
Source-First MaterialMacro, regulation and product content should prioritise official or professional sources, with source limits stated live.
Q&A BoundaryPersonal holdings, account-opening and specific product questions are redirected to general education, risk factors and appropriate advice channels.
Privacy and CommunicationRequest information is used for event matching, course communication and community notices, not personalised trading instructions.
Governance References ASIC: Giving financial product advice MoneySmart: General and personal financial advice
