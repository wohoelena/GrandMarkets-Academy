Knowledge Research Desk

The library is not a glossary. It is a market-understanding system

When learners enter the library, they need more than definitions. They need to know where each concept belongs: regulation sets boundaries, products shape risk, macro defines scenarios, platforms affect execution and review confirms learning.

01监管边界

先确认主体、授权、披露文件、投诉路径和一般教育边界，避免把品牌或牌照误读成投资结果保证。

02产品结构

把资产、合约、杠杆、保证金、点差、隔夜成本和流动性拆开，先理解风险如何形成。

03宏观情景

用通胀、就业、利率、美元、央行沟通和风险偏好建立观察顺序，而不是追逐单条新闻。

04平台与执行

理解订单、止损、滑点、交易时段、报价差异和模拟环境，让工具学习服务于风险管理。

05研究表达

把观点写成假设、依据、限制和更新条件，避免确定性、煽动性或个人化交易表达。

01Regulatory Boundary

Confirm entity, authorisation, disclosure documents, complaint paths and general-education boundaries before reading brand or licence information.

02Product Mechanics

Separate asset, contract, leverage, margin, spread, overnight cost and liquidity so learners understand how risk is formed.

03Macro Scenario

Use inflation, employment, rates, the US dollar, central-bank communication and risk appetite to build observation order.

04Platform and Execution

Understand orders, stops, slippage, trading sessions, quote differences and simulation so tools support risk management.

05Research Language

Write views as hypotheses, evidence, limitations and update conditions, avoiding certainty, hype or personalised trade language.

从资料来源到风险语言：GMA 知识库研究框架

金融知识不是孤立词条，而是一套从来源、结构、情景到复盘的阅读方法。

研究方法

每篇内容都按四层逻辑组织

学习者先知道信息来自哪里，再理解产品如何运作，随后放入宏观与市场情景，最后形成可复盘的风险语言。这样可以避免把新闻、观点和交易行为混在一起。

01资料来源

优先使用监管机构、央行、官方文档和专业机构资料，并标记内容的教育边界。

02产品结构

解释标的资产、合约结构、成本、杠杆、保证金、流动性和执行机制。

03市场情景

把利率、美元、通胀、就业、风险偏好和资金流动放入同一张观察表。

04风险复盘

记录假设、触发条件、失效条件和后续学习动作，而不是只记行情结论。

From source quality to risk language: the GMA knowledge framework

Financial knowledge is not a set of isolated definitions. It is a reading method from source, structure, scenario and review.

Research Method

Every topic follows a four-layer logic

Learners first identify where information comes from, then understand how the product works, place it in macro and market scenarios, and finally build reviewable risk language.

01Source Quality

Prioritise regulators, central banks, official documentation and professional institutions while marking the education boundary.

02Instrument Structure

Explain underlying assets, contract structure, costs, leverage, margin, liquidity and execution mechanics.

03Market Scenario

Place rates, the US dollar, inflation, employment, risk appetite and flows into one observation framework.

04Risk Review

Record assumptions, triggers, invalidation conditions and next learning actions rather than headline conclusions.

研究笔记模板

知识库内容可以直接沉淀为学习者自己的研究笔记，帮助他们用机构化方式记录市场问题。

步骤 01定义问题

先写清楚要理解的是监管规则、产品结构、宏观变量还是平台操作。

步骤 02确认来源

优先引用监管机构、央行、官方文档或专业机构资料，并记录发布时间。

步骤 03拆解机制

把概念拆成参与方、现金流、成本、风险和限制条件。

步骤 04放入情景

用利率、美元、通胀、就业、流动性或风险情绪解释可能的影响路径。

步骤 05写出边界

明确这只是一般教育或研究记录，不构成个人行动建议。

步骤 06复盘更新

在新数据、新监管资料或新市场事件出现后更新笔记。

Research Note Template

Knowledge Library content can be converted into a learner's own research notes, helping them record market questions in an institutional way.

Step 01Define the Question

Clarify whether the topic is regulation, product structure, macro variables or platform operation.

Step 02Confirm Sources

Prioritise regulators, central banks, official documentation or professional institutions and record publication dates.

Step 03Decompose Mechanics

Break the concept into participants, cash flows, costs, risks and limitations.

Step 04Place in Scenario

Use rates, the US dollar, inflation, employment, liquidity or risk sentiment to explain possible pathways.

Step 05State Boundaries

Clarify that the record is general education or research, not personal action advice.

Step 06Review and Update

Update the note when new data, regulatory materials or market events emerge.
