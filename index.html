市场学习的正确顺序

普通交易者最容易被行情吸引，但真正能长期进步的是先建立市场语言、风险框架和复盘方法。

先看结构标的资产、合约、交易时段、成本、流动性和监管边界。
再看情景宏观数据、央行政策、美元、利率、风险偏好和资金流动。
再看执行订单类型、滑点、点差、仓位、止损和退出条件。
最后复盘假设是否成立、风险是否超限、执行是否一致、下次如何改进。

The Right Sequence for Market Learning

Everyday traders are often pulled toward price action first. Long-term progress starts with market language, risk frameworks and review method.

Structure FirstUnderlying asset, contract, session, cost, liquidity and regulatory boundary.
Scenario NextMacro data, central-bank policy, the dollar, rates, risk appetite and flows.
Execution NextOrder type, slippage, spread, position size, stop and exit condition.
Review LastWhether thesis held, risk stayed within limit, execution matched plan and what improves next time.
