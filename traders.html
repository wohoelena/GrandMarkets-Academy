交易台框架

交易员训练不是信号推送，而是建立可验证的决策流程

公开资料显示，顶级交易机构的训练通常强调真实问题、模拟交易、导师反馈、研究和技术协作，以及在风险约束下快速学习。GMA 将这些原则应用于教育场景：训练市场理解、假设表达、风险预算、执行复盘和合规边界。

  • 把交易员训练和量化学院连接：一个训练判断与风险，一个训练工具与模型

六个交易员核心能力

我们不把“交易员”定义为预测价格的人，而是定义为能在不确定性下管理信息、风险和决策的人。

01

市场结构

理解产品、交易时段、流动性、订单类型、点差和不同市场参与者的行为。

02

概率思维

用分布、胜率、赔率、期望值和情景树表达判断，避免单点预测。

03

风险预算

在进入交易前定义最大亏损、仓位、失效条件、回撤限制和暂停规则。

04

执行纪律

训练入场、加减仓、止损、止盈、新闻波动和流动性变化下的操作边界。

05

复盘表达

把交易结果拆成假设、执行、风险、情绪和市场反馈，形成可改进记录。

06

团队协作

学习像交易台一样做晨会、风险会、研究共享和同伴反馈。

Six Core Trader Capabilities

We do not define a trader as someone who predicts price. A trader manages information, risk and decisions under uncertainty.

01

Market Structure

Understand products, sessions, liquidity, order types, spreads and the behaviour of different market participants.

02

Probabilistic Thinking

Use distributions, hit rate, payoff, expectancy and scenario trees instead of single-point forecasts.

03

Risk Allocation

Define maximum loss, sizing, invalidation, drawdown limits and pause rules before the trade.

04

Execution Discipline

Train entry, adjustment, stop, target, news volatility and liquidity-change boundaries.

05

Review Language

Break outcomes into thesis, execution, risk, emotion and market feedback to create an improvement record.

06

Desk Collaboration

Practise morning meetings, risk huddles, research sharing and peer feedback like a desk.

交易员手册

把交易想法写成可以被审查的训练手册

专业交易员不会只说“看涨”或“看跌”。更好的表达是：什么市场状态、什么触发条件、什么失效信号、允许承担多少风险，以及复盘时要交付什么记录。

训练手册适用情景触发条件失效条件风险纪律训练输出
宏观事件训练手册CPI、就业、央行利率决议、重要讲话。公布值相对预期出现显著偏离，且资产反应与逻辑一致。价格第一反应快速回撤，或相关资产没有确认。事件前写好最大亏损、滑点假设和暂停条件。事件前预案、事件后复盘、二次反应记录。
黄金 / 外汇情景训练手册美元、实际利率、避险需求和流动性共同变化。美元与收益率方向、风险情绪和关键价位形成一致情景。相关变量分化，或行情只由单一新闻驱动。避免用单一叙事解释所有波动，仓位随波动率调整。变量观察表、XAUUSD 情景树、风险预算表。
流动性与执行训练手册开盘、收盘、新闻发布、假期前后或流动性变薄时段。点差、成交速度、滑点和订单簿行为出现可观察变化。执行成本超出预案，或价格无法按计划成交。先降低交易频率和仓位，而不是提高杠杆追求结果。订单记录、滑点记录、执行后复盘。
趋势与均值回归训练手册行情进入持续趋势、区间震荡或假突破环境。趋势结构、波动率和成交行为支持同一交易逻辑。突破失败、成交量不确认、或波动结构改变。明确是否允许加仓，何时必须停止尝试。结构图、入场理由、失效线和复盘评分。
防守型风险训练手册连续亏损、极端波动、系统异常或情绪失控。触及日内亏损、连续错误、过度交易或无法解释的冲动。只有完成复盘、降低风险并重新定义计划后才恢复训练。暂停是训练的一部分，不是失败。暂停记录、错误索引、下一次训练目标。
触发条件允许进入观察或模拟执行的证据，不等于交易指令。
失效条件证明假设不再成立的条件，必须在执行前写清楚。
风险预算先定义最大损失、仓位和暂停规则，再讨论机会。
训练输出每次训练都要留下可复盘、可被导师检查的记录。
Trader Playbook

Turn trade ideas into reviewable playbooks

Professional traders do not stop at "bullish" or "bearish." Better language defines market state, trigger, invalidation, permitted risk and the record that must be reviewed afterward.

PlaybookScenarioTriggerInvalidationRisk DisciplineTraining Output
Macro Event PlaybookCPI, employment, central-bank rate decisions and major speeches.Release deviates materially from expectation and asset reaction confirms the logic.The first move retraces quickly, or related assets do not confirm.Define maximum loss, slippage assumption and pause condition before the event.Pre-event plan, post-event review and second-reaction log.
Gold / FX Scenario PlaybookUSD, real rates, safe-haven demand and liquidity change together.Dollar, yields, risk sentiment and key levels align into one scenario.Variables diverge, or the move is driven by a single headline.Avoid explaining all movement with one narrative; adjust size to volatility.Variable watch sheet, XAUUSD scenario tree and risk-budget sheet.
Liquidity and Execution PlaybookOpen, close, news release, holidays or thin-liquidity periods.Spread, fill speed, slippage and order-book behaviour show observable change.Execution cost exceeds plan, or price cannot be traded as planned.Reduce frequency and size before considering more leverage.Order record, slippage log and post-execution review.
Trend vs Mean-Reversion PlaybookPersistent trend, range regime or false-break environment.Structure, volatility and trade behaviour support the same logic.Breakout fails, volume does not confirm, or volatility regime changes.Define whether adding is allowed and when attempts must stop.Structure map, entry rationale, invalidation line and review score.
Defensive Risk PlaybookLosing streak, extreme volatility, system issue or emotional behaviour.Daily loss, repeated error, overtrading or unexplained impulse is reached.Training resumes only after review, lower risk and a redefined plan.Pausing is part of training, not failure.Pause record, error index and next training objective.
TriggerEvidence that allows observation or simulated execution; not a trading instruction.
InvalidationThe condition that proves the thesis no longer holds and must be written before execution.
Risk PlanDefine maximum loss, size and pause rule before discussing opportunity.
OutputEvery exercise leaves a reviewable record that can be checked by a mentor.

八阶段交易员训练框架

课程可按 8 个训练阶段搭建，每个阶段都有可交付成果，方便学员形成交易员作品集。

01市场与产品地图

外汇、黄金、指数、利率、股票和衍生品的结构、交易时段、成本和风险边界。

02概率与博弈训练

用期望值、赔率、分布和对手方行为理解交易不是预测，而是条件判断。

03风险预算与仓位

建立单笔风险、日内亏损、周度回撤、相关性暴露和暂停交易规则。

04宏观与催化剂

阅读 CPI、利率决议、央行讲话、就业数据和地缘事件对资产的影响。

05执行与订单流

训练挂单、止损、滑点、新闻波动、流动性缺口和执行后复盘。

06模拟交易台

在限定风险预算下进行情景演练，记录入场理由、调整动作和退出原因。

07交易日志与复盘

用标准模板评估是否遵守计划、是否超出风险、是否存在情绪化行为。

08交易员展示报告

输出一份包含研究、风险、执行、复盘和改进计划的交易员训练报告。

Eight-Stage Trader Development Framework

The course can be structured into eight training stages, each with a deliverable that helps learners build a trader portfolio.

01Market and Product Map

Structure, sessions, costs and risk boundaries across FX, gold, indices, rates, equities and derivatives.

02Probability and Game Training

Use expectancy, payoff, distributions and counterparty behaviour to understand trading as conditional judgement.

03Risk Budget and Sizing

Build rules for single-trade risk, daily loss, weekly drawdown, correlation exposure and trading pauses.

04Macro and Catalysts

Read CPI, rate decisions, central-bank speeches, employment data and geopolitical events through asset impact.

05Execution and Order Flow

Train limit orders, stops, slippage, news volatility, liquidity gaps and post-execution review.

06Simulation Desk

Run scenario exercises under a defined risk budget, recording entry rationale, adjustments and exit reasons.

07Trade Journal and Review

Use a standard template to assess plan discipline, risk breaches and emotional behaviour.

08Trader Presentation Report

Produce a report covering research, risk, execution, review and an improvement plan.

训练晋级

从市场观察者到模拟交易台成员

专业训练不应让学习者一开始就追求实盘结果，而是用明确门槛逐步验证研究、风险和执行能力。

晋级标准优先看流程，不优先看短期盈亏

每一层训练都要求学习者提交可检查的材料。只有当研究记录、风险预算、执行纪律和复盘质量稳定后，才进入更复杂的模拟情景。

L0市场观察者

能阅读日历、识别主要资产、记录来源和区分新闻与市场反应。

L1研究候选人

能写出情景树、变量关系、关键数据和明确的失效条件。

L2模拟执行员

能在模拟环境中按计划执行，并完整记录订单、调整和退出原因。

L3风险纪律员

能遵守单笔、单日和连续亏损规则，并主动暂停而不是冲动补偿。

L4交易台展示者

能向导师或同伴清楚解释研究、风险、执行、错误和下一步改进。

Training Ladder

From market observer to simulation-desk contributor

Professional development should not push learners toward live outcomes first. It should use clear gates to verify research, risk and execution capability.

Promotion standards prioritise process over short-term P&L

Each level requires reviewable material. More complex simulation scenarios are introduced only after research records, risk budgets, execution discipline and review quality become consistent.

L0Market Observer

Reads calendars, identifies major assets, records sources and separates news from market reaction.

L1Research Candidate

Writes scenario trees, variable relationships, key data points and explicit invalidation conditions.

L2Simulation Executor

Executes according to plan in simulation and records orders, adjustments and exit reasons.

L3Risk Disciplined Operator

Respects trade, daily and losing-streak rules and pauses proactively instead of compensating emotionally.

L4Desk Presenter

Explains research, risk, execution, errors and next improvements clearly to mentors or peers.

Improvement loop
交易日志

交易员日志模板：把情绪变成数据，把错误变成训练题

交易日志不是为了证明自己对，而是为了找到流程中最值得改进的一环。

日志阶段必须回答的问题高质量信号危险信号
交易前为什么现在关注？情景树是什么？最大亏损是多少？有来源、有变量、有失效条件、有仓位理由。只写“感觉要涨/跌”，没有退出计划。
执行中是否按计划入场？是否因情绪临时改变仓位或止损？所有调整都有记录，并能说明原因。连续下单、追价、扩大亏损或取消原计划。
交易后结果来自好流程还是坏流程？市场反馈支持还是否定假设？能区分结果、过程、情绪和市场环境。只看盈亏，盈利就忽略错误，亏损就归因市场。
周度复盘重复错误是什么？哪些规则需要修改？下周只训练哪一件事？形成错误索引、风险统计和下一周训练目标。复盘泛泛而谈，没有可执行的改进动作。
Trade Journal

Trader journal template: turn emotion into data and errors into drills

The trade journal is not used to prove that the learner was right. It identifies the part of the process most worth improving.

Journal StageRequired QuestionQuality SignalRed Flag
Before TradeWhy focus now? What is the scenario tree? What is the maximum loss?Sources, variables, invalidation and sizing rationale are present.Only says "feels up/down" with no exit plan.
During ExecutionWas entry according to plan? Did emotion change size or stop?Every adjustment is recorded and explained.Repeated orders, chasing price, expanding loss or abandoning the plan.
After TradeDid the result come from good or bad process? Did market feedback confirm or reject the thesis?Outcome, process, emotion and market environment are separated.Only reviewing P&L; ignoring errors after gains or blaming the market after losses.
Weekly ReviewWhat repeated error appeared? Which rule needs revision? What single drill matters next week?Error index, risk statistics and next-week objective are produced.Generic review with no executable improvement action.

交易员评分卡

训练不以短期盈亏作为唯一指标，而看决策质量、风险纪律和复盘可改进性。

研究质量

是否能用来源、数据和情景树支持交易假设。

风险纪律

是否提前定义风险预算，并在压力中遵守边界。

执行一致性

是否按计划执行，而不是临场追涨杀跌。

复盘深度

是否能把错误整理为下一次训练目标。

Trader Scorecard

Training should not use short-term P&L as the only metric. It should assess decision quality, risk discipline and review depth.

Research Quality

Whether sources, data and scenario trees support the trade thesis.

Risk Discipline

Whether risk budget is defined in advance and respected under pressure.

Execution Consistency

Whether actions follow the plan rather than chasing price in the moment.

Review Depth

Whether errors can become clear objectives for the next training round.

新手交易者常见误区与专业训练替代方案

真正的交易员训练不是强化冲动，而是把不确定性拆成可管理的流程。

常见误区为什么危险专业训练替代输出物
只找入场点忽略失效条件、流动性和退出计划。先写情景树：看多、看空、中性分别需要什么证据。交易假设卡。
连续亏损后加仓把情绪反应包装成“摊低成本”，容易突破风险预算。预先定义单日最大亏损、连续亏损暂停和复盘条件。风险预算表。
把消息当指令新闻本身不等于市场反应，关键是相对预期和仓位状态。记录事件前预期、公布值、首轮反应和二次反应。事件复盘表。
只看盈亏复盘盈利也可能来自坏流程，亏损也可能来自好流程。把复盘拆成研究、风险、执行、情绪和市场反馈。交易员评分卡。
无计划使用杠杆小幅波动可能造成超出预期的账户变化。按波动率和最大承受损失反推仓位，而不是按信心大小下单。仓位计算记录。

交易员训练与量化学院如何并列

两个方向训练不同能力，但应该互相连接，形成“判断 + 工具”的完整训练路径。

训练方向核心能力训练方式适合人群
交易员训练学院市场判断、风险预算、执行纪律、复盘表达。模拟交易台、情景推演、交易日志、风险会。想训练交易决策、职业交易员思维和风险纪律的人。
量化 EA 编程学院策略编码、回测、数据处理、自动化执行。MQL5、MT5 EA、Python、策略作品集。想把交易想法整理为程序、模型和研究工具的人。
共同边界都不承诺收益，不提供个人交易建议。教育训练、模拟、研究和风险意识。愿意把市场学习做成长期训练流程的人。
